Ginevra Castellano

Associate Senior Lecturer in Intelligent Interactive Systems
PhD, Docent (Associate Professor)
Department of Information Technlogy
Uppsala University, Sweden

Since October 2014 I am an Associate Senior Lecturer in Intelligent Interactive Systems at the Department of Information Technology of Uppsala University, Sweden, where I lead the Social Robotics Lab. My research aims to design and develop robots that learn to interact socially with humans and bring benefits to the society we live in, for example in application areas such as education, e-learning, healthcare and assistive technology. By adopting an interdisciplinary approach grounded in principles from the social sciences, I aim to develop computational social abilities that allow robots to behave in a socially intelligent way.
