Uncanny Valley Effect

In this project, we study the uncanny valley effect. Within the area, I'm looking into the interplay between interaction strategy, appearance and the feelings people have while interacting with a robot.

Social Learning

In social learning projects (for example EMOTE), we researched robot human interaction principles. We developed several algorithms on robots to enable robot to teach young teenagers in a more natural way than earlier technologies.
